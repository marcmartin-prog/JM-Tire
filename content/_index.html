About Us

We are a small, family-owned business, new to the North Iowa community, but not new to the tire industry. From starting as a tire technician at age 17, to starting a full-time business in 2017, to relocating to northern Iowa, we have gone through an incredible journey! After running JM Tire near Antigo, WI for four years, we as a family decided to move to Saint Ansgar IA in 2021, where we purchased the Wold's service shop from the Wold family. The transition has gone well and our goal is to serve you with the legacy that we earned in Wisconsin, as well as the legacy left to us by the Wold's!

We are a young, growing family. My wife, Helga and I have three little girls, Tirzah, Vienna, and Kyrie. Our greatest goal in life is to serve God, and we strive to do that by serving our neighbors and friends!
